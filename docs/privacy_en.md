# Privacy Policy

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information Collected Directly by the Developer
The App does not transmit any personal information or match data to the Developer's servers. All match records (scores, event logs, etc.) are stored locally on your device.

### Information Collected by Third-Party Services
The App uses the following third-party services, which may collect information according to their own policies.

| Service | Purpose | Policy |
|---------|---------|--------|
| Google AdMob | Ad delivery (free version only) | [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) |
| Apple App Store | In-app purchase processing | [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/) |

---

## 2. Advertising

- The free version of the App displays ads provided by Google AdMob.
- AdMob may use device identifiers (such as IDFA) to serve relevant ads.
- You can disable app tracking in iOS via **Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking**.
- After purchasing the ad removal feature, the AdMob SDK will no longer make ad requests.

---

## 3. Purchases

- The ad removal purchase is processed through Apple's App Store.
- The Developer does not collect or store any payment information such as credit card numbers.
- Purchase history is managed by Apple and associated with your Apple ID.

---

## 4. Data Storage

| Data Type | Storage Location | External Transmission |
|-----------|-----------------|----------------------|
| Match records & event logs | On-device (SwiftData) | None |
| Purchase status (isPro flag) | On-device (UserDefaults) | None |
| CSV export files | On-device temporary folder | Only when shared by user |
